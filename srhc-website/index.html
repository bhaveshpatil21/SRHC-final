Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have home visits?+

Yes. We provide home visits by qualified General Physicians for patients unable to travel. Book a home visit via call or WhatsApp and the doctor will visit at your convenience.

Do you accept walk-in patients?+

Yes, walk-ins are welcome. However, we recommend booking an appointment in advance to avoid waiting and ensure faster consultation.

Do you provide emergency care?+

We offer basic and urgent medical support, including first aid, ECG, minor procedures and immediate doctor attention. For major emergencies, we guide patients to the nearest hospital immediately.

Can I get blood tests done at the clinic?+

Absolutely. We offer comprehensive diagnostic testing in partnership with Lupin Diagnostics, including routine tests, specialised profiles and customised health packages.

What are your clinic timings?+

We are open 9am to 9pm Monday to Saturday to care for you.
